Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Statistics:
Prep - 30 min
Cook - 15 min
Ready in 45 min
Makes 21 Servings


Ingredients:
½ cup butter, softened
¾ cup packed light brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
1 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
½ tsp baking powder
¼ tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1-½ cups quick-cooking oats
½ cup semisweet chocolate chips


Directions:
Preheat the oven to 335 degrees F.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth, light, and fluffy.
Beat in egg, then stir in vanilla.
Combine the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; stir into the creamed mixture until just blended.
Mix in the quick oats and chocolate chips.
Chill dough in the freezer for 10 min or in the fridge for 30 min.
Drop by heaping spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake for 12 minutes in the preheated oven.
Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
